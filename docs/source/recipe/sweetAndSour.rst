*************
Sweet And Sour Chicken
*************

Overview
########

* Servings: 4
* Prep Time: less than 30 minutes
* Cook Time: 10–30 minutes

Ingredients
###########

Chicken
#######

* ½ small pineapple
* 2 boneless skinless chicken breasts
* 30 ml sunflower oil (2 tbsp)
* 1 onion, cut into wedges
* 1 red pepper, deseeded and chopped
* 1 green pepper, deseeded and chopped
* 8 g cornflour (1 tbsp)
* 4 spring onions, trimmed and finely diagonally sliced

Sauce

#####

* 8 g cornflour (1 tbsp)
* 300 ml fresh pineapple juice (10 fl oz)
* 2 garlic cloves, crushed
* 25 g fresh root ginger, peeled and finely grated (1 oz) ** REDUCE TO 15 g (1/2 oz) IF YOU PREFER A MILD FLAVOUR **
* 15 ml dark soy sauce (1 tbsp)
* 30 ml white wine vinegar (2 tbsp)
* 25 g soft light brown sugar (2 tbsp)
* 45 ml tomato ketchup (3 tbsp)
* 1 pinch dried chilli flakes
* freshly ground black pepper

Instructions
############

* Place the cornflour for the sauce in a small bowl and mix with 30 ml pineapple juice (2 tbsp) until smooth.

* In a separate bowl combine the remaining pineapple juice, garlic, ginger, soy sauce, vinegar, sugar, tomato ketchup and chilli flakes. Mix well and set aside.

* Cut the skin from the pineapple. Quarter lengthways, remove the core and cut the flesh into thick chunks. Cut each chicken breast into 7–9 evenly sized pieces.

* Heat the sunflower oil in a large frying pan or wok over high heat. Stir-fry the onion and peppers for 3 minutes.

* Coat the chicken pieces in the cornflour, add to the pan and stir-fry for about 4 minutes until lightly coloured on all sides.

* Add the pineapple chunks and the prepared sauce. Bring to a simmer over medium heat and cook for 4–5 minutes, stirring regularly, until the chicken is cooked through and the pineapple is hot.

* Stir in the cornflour and pineapple juice mixture and cook for 30–60 seconds until the sauce thickens and becomes glossy, coating the chicken and vegetables evenly.

* Sprinkle with the sliced spring onions and serve hot.
