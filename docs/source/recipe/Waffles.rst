*************
Waffles
*************

Ingredients
##########

* 250g plain flour
* 1 teaspoon salt
* 4 teaspoons baking powder
* 2 tablespoons caster sugar
* 2 eggs
* 355ml warm milk
* 75g butter, melted
* 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Method
######

#. Mix all the ingredients
#. Pour mixture into Waffle Maker for 6-7 minutes or until the steam stops coming out of the maker.
